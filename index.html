About Instant Loans

Our Loan Servicing Plan.

Our loan servicing plan outlines the processes and procedures for managing crypto loans on our platform. Our goal is to provide efficient, secure, and transparent loan servicing to ensure a positive experience for our borrowers and investors.

1. Loan Disbursal: Funds are disbursed to borrowers' wallets upon loan approval.
2. Monthly Payments: Borrowers make monthly payments in crypto, which are distributed to investors.
3. Interest and Principal: Payments are allocated to interest and principal according to the loan agreement.
4. Late Payment Handling: Late payments trigger automated reminders and potential penalties.
5. Loan Status Updates: Regular updates are provided to borrowers and investors on loan status and payment history.
6. Security and Custody: Crypto assets are held in secure, insured wallets to ensure asset protection.
7. Compliance and Reporting: Regular reporting and compliance with regulatory requirements.
8. Customer Support: Dedicated support team for borrowers and investors.
